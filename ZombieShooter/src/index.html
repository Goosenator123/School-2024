Zombie Shooter 3D

🎮 Controls

  • Move: Use W / A / S / D keys to move around.
  • Aim: Move your mouse to look around. The mouse will lock to the screen during play.
  • Shoot: Click the left mouse button to shoot.

🧟 Objective

Survive waves of zombies by shooting them before they reach you. Each kill earns 10 points. Be careful — not all zombies are the same:

  • Green Zombies – Normal type, balanced speed and health.
  • Red Zombies – Fast type, move quickly but have low health.
  • Dark Green Zombies – Tank type, slow but with high health.

❤️ Health System

Your health starts at 100. When zombies get close, they deal damage. The game ends when your health drops to 0, showing your final score.

🔊 Music

Background music begins when the game starts and stops upon death. Make sure your sound is on!

🧱 Environment

  • Randomly generated walls block both player and zombies.
  • Zombies find their way around obstacles to reach you.
  • Use the map’s layout to your advantage and avoid getting cornered.

Game Over

Score: 0 | Health: 100
