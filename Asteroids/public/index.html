Asteroids

Controls: Use the arrow keys to rotate and thrust, and press the spacebar to shoot.
Buffs Available:
M (Multi) – Increases the number of bullets per shot.
B (Bullet Speed) – Increases bullet speed.
P (Penetration) – Increases bullet penetration.
R (Reload Speed) – Decreases the ship's fire rate (reload time).
Points: Each asteroid destroyed gives 10 points multiplied by the current multiplier.
