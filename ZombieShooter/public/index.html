Zombie Shooter 3D

Instructions

  • Move: Use WASD keys to move the player.
  • Aim: Move the mouse to look around (mouse locks to center on play).
  • Shoot: Click to fire bullets at zombies.
  • Objective: Kill zombies (3 hits each, 10 points per kill). Avoid getting hit to keep health above 0.
  • Game Over: If health reaches 0, view your score and return to the menu.
